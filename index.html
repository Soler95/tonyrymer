Biography

The Artist

Tony Rymer is an internationally performing cellist known for expressive tone, refined interpretation and commanding stage presence across Europe and the United States. His playing bridges the classical tradition with a deeply personal voice.

A graduate of the Royal Academy of Music, Rymer has performed in the world's most prestigious concert halls alongside leading orchestras and chamber ensembles, bringing audiences to their feet from Berlin to New York.
